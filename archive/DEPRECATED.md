# 🗄️ The Cold Brew Archive: Deprecated Examples

Welcome to the **Cold Brew Archive**! 🗄️ These are the legacy beans and recipes that were once the daily roast but have since been replaced by newer, more refined methods (like **Cloud Workstations**).

We keep these here for historical reference only. They are like that vintage espresso machine in the corner—full of memories, but perhaps not the one you want to use for today's high-demand tasks.

### ☕ What's in the Archive?
*   **Cloud Shell**: The original "instant coffee" of GCP development.
*   **GitPod**: A classic third-party roast that we've now traded for native GCP solutions.
